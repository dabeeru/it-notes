- Managed SaaS Graph database provided by [neo4j](https://neo4j.com/)
- Available on [[AWS]] and [[GCP]]
- Support query language called [[Cypher]]
- Support for wide variety of lanugages 
- Connectors to [[Spark]] and [[Kafka]]
